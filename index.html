• Roll one die for your first turn. Playing with two dice is optional
  • Enter points on the score sheet if the number rolled is two, three,
    four, five, or six points. If you roll a one, you lose all the points you
    accumulated during that turn, and you lose your turn.
  • Continue rolling the die until you're happy with the number of points
    you've scored. Then, "hold" by handing the die over to your opponent, and track
    all points earned during your turn.
  • Reach 100 points first and win the game.
